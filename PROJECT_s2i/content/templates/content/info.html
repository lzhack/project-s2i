How it works?



First step. Create.

If you want to post on chain your content immediately remember to check the box "On Chain". Otherwise save it for a second time, but remember that you will have to communicate us when you want to post it on chain because only we will be able to do it.

Main part, the magic happens. PostOnChain.

Once created, a "Hash" of the content and an "Id" of the transaction will be assigned to the post, which will allow its content to be on the blockchain.

And lastly. Check.

Your message is in blockchain. If you want to check that everything went well, just log on to "https://ropsten.etherscan.io/", enter your "Transaction Id" and immediately check the presence of the content of your post.
